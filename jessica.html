    • Jessica Reilly, Sophomore

    Jessica, also known as Jess or as her teamates like to call her, Betsy. Jess throws in almost every event. In the winter she is our top weight thrower and dabbles in shot put. In the outdoor spring season she spends her time throwing three events for us, hammer, discus and shot put.

    Achievements

    Freshman Year

    • Finished the weight season third in TCNJ history and placed 5th in the NJAC Indoor Conference Championship
    • AARTFC regional qualifier for the weight throw
    • At the NJAC Outdoor Conference Championship she finished 6th in the hammer throw and 7th in shot put.
    • AARTFC regional qualifier for shot put in the outdoor season.

    Sophomore Year

    • BROKE THE TCNJ WEIGHT SCHOOL RECORD WITH A THROW OF 15.75m
    • Finished 4th in weight throw and 5th in shot put at the NJAC Indoor Conference Championship
    • Headed back to AARTFC Regional Championship for weight.
    • Breaks another school record in the Hammer throw with a toss of 49.26m, qualifying for AARTFC Regional championship for the first time in Hammer.
    • Headed back to AARTFC Regional Championship for shot put as well with a throw of 11.71 m.
